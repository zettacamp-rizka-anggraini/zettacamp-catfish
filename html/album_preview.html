Love Our Self

Yura Yunita's lyrics also raise the confidence of many women. Which reminds us that perfection belongs only to God. The lyrics also describe it as a process of trying to be grateful for ourselves no matter what the circumstances. No need for acknowledgment from others. Realizing that you are very valuable and able to make your own way when someone tries to cover it up. Differences in skin color, hair type, physical deficiencies and other differences are not a barrier to not being confident. Basically all women are beautiful.
